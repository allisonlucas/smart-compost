Maintaining an Ideal Content Ratioclose

An effective compost pile is a careful balance of dry or brown material that contains carbon (like leaves or paper) and wet or green things that contain nitrogen (like food scraps or rabbit droppings). If you add a lot of shredded paper or cardboard to the pile, you will need to balance it with a nice heap of fresh grass clippings or horse manure.
